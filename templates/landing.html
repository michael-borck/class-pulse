Everything you need to engage a room

Built for presenters who want a pulse on the room

ClassPulse is a real-time audience response tool. Run a quick check-for-understanding mid-lecture, gather feedback in a workshop, or run an interactive quiz — all from one lightweight dashboard.

⚡

Real-time, over WebSockets

Responses appear the moment they're submitted. No refreshing, no waiting — see the room react live.

📱

Join in seconds

Every session gets an auto-generated QR code and shareable link. Audience members join from any phone or laptop — no account needed.

📊

Live result visualizations

Bar charts, word clouds, rating breakdowns, ranking order, and side-by-side image tallies render instantly and stay in sync as responses arrive.

🗂️

Nine question types

Multiple choice, select-all, ranking, numeric, rating, short answer, word cloud, image choice, and poll-with-other — mix them freely in one session.

🙋

Let the room ask

In cohort mode your audience anonymously proposes questions and upvotes each other's. Proposals pass a keyword filter before they surface, and flagged ones wait for your approval.

✨

AI-assisted drafting optional

Stuck on wording? Draft and refine questions with an AI provider you configure — or leave it switched off entirely.

⬇️

Export to CSV

Download raw responses per question or per session for grading, analysis, or your records.

🗃️

Sessions & archive

Organise questions into sessions, reuse them, and archive the ones you're done with — without losing the data.

Nine question types, endless uses

The right question for the moment

Mix and match within a single session to keep your audience engaged and surface exactly the insight you need.

Choice

Polls & comprehension checks

Multiple choice for quick votes and knowledge checks, select-all when more than one answer fits, or poll-with-other to catch the option you didn't think of.

Open text

Open reflection

Collect free-text responses and read them as a list, or watch a word cloud form in real time — great for brainstorming and sentiment.

Scales

Structured feedback

Rating scales to gauge confidence or agreement, numeric answers with an optional range, and ranking to have the room order a set of items.

Image choice

Visual decisions

Upload images or link to your own, then let the audience vote. Results show the images themselves with live tallies — ideal for design critique and spot-the-difference.

From zero to live in under a minute

How it works

No installs for your audience. You build once; they just scan and respond.

Create a session

From your dashboard, spin up a session and add a few questions — a poll, a word cloud, a rating scale, or any of the nine types.

Audience joins

Share the QR code or session link. Participants enter a short code and they're in — no sign-up required.

Watch results stream live

Open a question and responses flow in instantly. Export to CSV whenever you're done.
